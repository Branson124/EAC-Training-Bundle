# Buyer model & pilot profile

## Primary model (recommended for Phase B → C)

**Hybrid: annual license + optional SaaS uplift**

| Layer | What the buyer pays for | What you deliver |
|-------|-------------------------|------------------|
| **License (base)** | Right to use the **methodology, playbooks, and field assets** for one legal entity (contractor), named users or site count in contract | Documented process, PDFs, curriculum, CRM static build or white-label static URL |
| **SaaS uplift (optional)** | **Per-seat or per-company** subscription when they need **shared cloud CRM**, manager dashboards, and completion logs | Hosted app with auth, multi-tenant data, backups (Phase C) |

**Why this hybrid**

- **License-first** matches where the IP lives today (static CRM + files)—low engineering cost for first paid pilots.
- **SaaS uplift** aligns revenue with **ongoing hosting, support, and compliance updates** without forcing every small contractor into a subscription on day one.

**Alternative (simpler for very first pilot)**

- **Pilot = single annual license + white-glove onboarding** (no subscription). Add SaaS when the second customer insists on shared team data.

## Pilot contractor profile (target for Phase B)

Use this ICP to choose the first case study.

| Dimension | Ideal pilot |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Size** | 5–30 person sales + intake team (big enough to feel pain; small enough to decide fast) |
| **Offer** | Home performance, insulation/HVAC, or energy upgrade work aligned with **state / utility programs** (e.g. North Carolina program literacy) |
| **Pain** | Inconsistent rep talk track, leads stuck in spreadsheets, managers cannot see pipeline stage |
| **Champion** | Owner or sales director who can mandate **one CRM + one process** for 30–60 days |
| **Tech** | Comfortable with a **web app** and **mobile browsers** in the field; no custom IT required |
| **Geography** | Carolinas or adjacent (so your talk tracks and sources stay credible); expand later with localized PDF variants |

**Anti-pilot (avoid for v1)**

- No single champion; everyone “optional” on the tool.
- Expecting **guaranteed program outcomes** from your training (manage expectations in contract).
- Needs **full LMS + HRIS integration** before trying a checklist CRM.

## Commercial checklist (before first invoice)

- [ ] Entity name and **license scope** (users, territories, duration).
- [ ] **Disclaimer**: training reflects Energy Advocate Carolinas **process**; official eligibility comes from **current program and utility rules**.
- [ ] **Support boundaries**: email/office hours; what is **not** included (legal advice, utility disputes).
- [ ] **Data**: for static CRM, clarify **device-local storage** vs. future cloud (see Phase C doc).
